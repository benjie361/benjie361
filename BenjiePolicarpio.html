About Me

College for Research and Technology

The College for Research and Technology (CRT) is a private educational institution in the Philippines that offers quality, affordable education in various fields such as Information Technology, Business Administration, Criminology, Hospitality Management, and Education. Known for its hands-on and practical approach to learning, CRT aims to produce industry-ready graduates equipped with both theoretical knowledge and real-world skills. Bachelor’s of Science in Information Technology (CRT)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program at the College for Research and Technology (CRT) is designed to equip students with strong technical skills and a solid understanding of modern computing technologies. The program focuses on both theory and hands-on training in areas such as programming, web development, database management, networking, and systems analysis. CRT ensures that BSIT students are industry-ready by providing practical experiences, updated curricula, and exposure to real-world IT environments. Graduates of this program are prepared to take on careers in software development, IT support, cybersecurity, systems administration, and more--making them competitive in the ever-evolving field of technology.
